About Me

I'm a TESOL Master's graduate from the UK, returning to China to work as an English teacher in an international school or university setting. My academic interests sit at the intersection of second language acquisition, learner motivation, and the rapidly evolving role of AI in language education.

I designed this website to serve two purposes: a professional portfolio for job applications and PhD admissions, and a practical resource hub for the Chinese English learners and fellow teachers I work with. I believe teaching and research should inform each other — and that good teaching materials should always be grounded in what the evidence actually says about how people learn.

I am planning to pursue a PhD in 2027–2028, with a proposed focus on L2 motivational trajectories in the age of generative AI, or on the use of AI tools for personalised vocabulary instruction.
